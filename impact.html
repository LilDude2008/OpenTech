Impact

Repair and reuse create real community value.

Keeping technology in use longer can help people stay connected and keep more devices out of landfills.

Less waste Fewer devices are thrown away too soon.
More access People keep using the technology they already have.
More confidence Clear support helps people solve problems with less stress.

Why this matters for people

  • Repair can be more affordable than replacing a device.
  • Simple guidance can prevent small problems from becoming bigger ones.
  • Learning basic device care helps people stay independent.

Why this matters for the environment

Choosing reuse over replacement helps reduce electronic waste and supports more responsible technology habits.

OpenTech supports people and the planet at the same time.

Small repairs, clear information, and longer device life can make a meaningful difference.

OpenTech works toward a future where useful technology is repaired, understood, and reused.
