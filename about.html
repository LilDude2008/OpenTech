About OpenTech

We help people feel more confident with technology.

OpenTech is a nonprofit project built around a simple idea: useful devices should stay useful for longer.

What we do

We connect people with practical help for device problems, repair choices, and tech basics.

Why we exist

Many people replace devices because repair feels confusing, expensive, or out of reach.

Who we support

We focus on community members, families, students, and anyone who wants simpler tech support.

Our approach

We keep information short, clear, and welcoming.

  • Explain problems in plain language.
  • Encourage repair before replacement.
  • Share skills people can use again later.

What guides us

Technology should support people, not overwhelm them.

When people understand their devices better, they can save money, reduce waste, and stay connected.

OpenTech believes repair and understanding should feel possible for everyone.
